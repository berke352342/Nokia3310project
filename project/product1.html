Nokia 3310 (2000 Original)

The Nokia 3310 was released in the year 2000 and quickly became one of the most iconic mobile phones of all time. Known for its incredible durability, long-lasting battery, and classic games like Snake, it sold over 126 million units worldwide.

  • Release Year: 2000
  • Features: Monochrome display, SMS chat, removable battery, Snake II game
  • Battery Life: Up to 260 hours standby, 4.5 hours talk time
  • Storage: 10 SMS messages, 8 dialed/received/missed calls
  • Price: ~$166 USD (at launch)
