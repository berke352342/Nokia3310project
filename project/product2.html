Nokia 3310 (2017 Re-release)

The Nokia 3310 was reintroduced in 2017 as a modern take on the classic phone. It retained the beloved design and durability while adding color display, a basic camera, and updated software. This release captured the nostalgia of the original with new-day features.

  • Release Year: 2017
  • Features: Color screen, 2MP camera, updated Snake game, longer battery life
  • Connectivity: 2G network support
  • Price: Approximately $50 - $60 at launch
