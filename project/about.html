About the Company

Nokia was founded in 1865 in Finland as a paper mill. Over the decades, it evolved into a global leader in telecommunications and mobile technology.

One of Nokia’s most iconic products was the Nokia 3310, released in 2000. Known for its legendary durability, long battery life, and the addictive game Snake, the 3310 became one of the best-selling phones of all time.

Nokia’s journey from a small paper company to a mobile phone legend is a story of innovation, resilience, and trust. The Nokia 3310 remains a symbol of reliability and timeless design, remembered fondly by millions around the world.
